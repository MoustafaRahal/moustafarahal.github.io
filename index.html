Ibrahim Sbeity

Biography

Ibrahim Sbeity is a second-year PhD student currently affiliated with the French Commission of Atomic Energy (CEA-Leti). He holds a Bachelor's degree in Electrical and Communication Engineering from Phoenicia University, Lebanon. Following his undergraduate studies, he pursued his Master's degree in Paris-Saclay University, France, specializing in the field of machine learning and control.
Throughout his academic journey, Ibrahim consistently demonstrated a deep interest in the domains of Machine Learning and Localization, which was evident through his various projects. Building upon his strong foundation, he embarked on his current doctoral research titled "Deep Wireless Localization Using Artificial Intelligence for Challenging Environments" at the Doctoral School of Cergy-Paris University. Ibrahim conducts his research at CEA-Leti, where he explores innovative approaches to wireless localization using cutting-edge techniques in artificial intelligence.
