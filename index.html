Moustafa Rahal

Biography

Moustafa Rahal is a Research Engineer at the University of Surrey, UK, specializing in Digital Signal Processing and Wireless Communication Systems. After successfully pursuing his bachelor's and masters's degrees in "Computer Engineering and Architecture" and "Computer and Communications Engineering" respectively, Moustafa commenced his Ph.D. in "Radio-based mmWave Localization using Reconfigurable Intelligent Surfaces (RIS) for 6G". During his thesis, Moustafa had the chance to act as a research visitor at the Chalmers University of Technology as part of the EU H2020 Rise-6G project, where he worked with the CROSSNET Team its leader Prof. Henk Wymeersch. He also co-authored many of the project's reports and deliverables. Moreover, he has contributed to the academic community by successfully publishing his work as papers in high-impact conference proceedings and prestigious transactions. Additionally, his background in Digital Signal Processing is enforced and diversified by actively accomplishing various online trainings in the fields of machine learning and deep learning.
